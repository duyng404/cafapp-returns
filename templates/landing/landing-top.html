How It Works

Get Delivery Cards from the Bookmark

The CafApp delivery cards used to pay for your deliveries are sold at the Bookmark. Get one before you order!

Log in & Build Your Order

No need to sign up, log in to CafApp right away using your GusMail account and start your order. Choose from a selection of Burger, Pizza, Chicken strips and Sandwiches. And did we mention free sides and drinks?

Pay using your Caf Account

Your food will be charged directly to your caf account's declining balance, while the delivery fee is paid by the delivery cards from the Bookmark.

Pick up food at the dorms

We currently deliver to these select dorms: CoEd, Complex, Uhler, Rundstrom, Pittman, Sohre and Southwest. We take the walking out of dinner and late night snacks!
